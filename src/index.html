SenseUI provides real-time AI feedback to blind and low vision developers.

How to use:

  1. Open the webpage you want feedback on.
  2. To open SenseUI, press Ctrl+Shift+S on windows or Command+Shift+S on Mac.
  3. Ask a question on the chat or type / to use the commands.

SenseUI is still in beta and AI feedback might be inaccurate. Please report any issues.
